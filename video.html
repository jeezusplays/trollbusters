Symptoms that you are toxic (video evidence)

Signs of toxic people

Trolling: Toxic players sometimes engage in trolling behavior, such as intentionally disrupting the game or intentionally feeding the enemy team.

Excessive Use of Caps Lock or Exclamation Marks: They may use caps lock or excessive exclamation marks to express anger or frustration, which can come across as aggressive.

Excessive Trash Talking: Toxic players often engage in excessive trash talking, which can include offensive language, insults, and derogatory comments. They may target other players with personal attacks.
