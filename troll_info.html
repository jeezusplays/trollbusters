Fighting Online Trolls

Together, we can make the internet a better place.

What are online trolls?

Online trolls are individuals who post offensive, inflammatory, or disruptive comments on the internet to provoke others. They can be hurtful and disruptive.

How to combat trolls?

1. Don't engage: Avoid responding to trolls. They thrive on attention.

2. Report and block: Most platforms have reporting and blocking features. Use them.

3. Support others: Stand up for victims and provide a safe space.

Spread kindness

Let's make the internet a better place by spreading kindness and positivity. Share compliments and support others.
